Home (current) Clients How to use

Use this site to track the amount of time you spend working on a client!

Follow the instructions below to get going

  • Create a new client by clicking on the + Add New Client button on either the Home page or the Clients page.
  • Once you have at least one client, you will be able to add that client to today's time card on the Home page.
  • Choose the client you want to add from the dropdown and click the "Add client to today" button. This will add the client and a timer to today's time card.
  • You can add as many clients as you like to today's time card. It is recommended that you only add clients when you know you will be working on them, to help save space!
  • Once you have at least one clien added to today's time card, you can click on that client and the timer will start. You will see the seconds increasing and you're ready to start tracking your time.
  • Click on another client to start the timer for that client, or if you're taking a break/finished for the day, click on the current active client and the timer will stop.
  • Your timesheet data will be saved in this browser (make sure to use the same browser!) and needs to be cleared every week after you've successfully taken down all the times. If you don't clear the browser's local storage, you might end up double counting your time.
Have fun!
